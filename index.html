Who We Are

Not New. Not Shiny. Still Here.

WyldWare.Com opened its doors in 1996 and hasn't closed them since. Three decades hosting MUD, MUX, MUSH, and MOO games for players who take their worlds seriously.

We've seen it all: market crashes, tech bubbles, hosting companies burning bright and going bust. Through all of it, we kept hosting. Because we're not chasing trends — we're keeping promises.

If you've been playing text games since the '90s, you already know what we do. If you're new to the genre, you're in good hands. We've been doing this longer than most people have been online.
