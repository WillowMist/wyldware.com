Common Questions

How long does setup take?

Most games are up and running within 24–48 hours of subscribing. Custom setups or unusual configurations may take a bit longer — we'll let you know upfront.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. Start with what fits your game now and scale up (or down) as needed. Just reach out and we'll handle it.

What if I need something not listed?

That's what the custom package option is for. Tell us what you're running — we'll figure out a plan that works.

Do you support Evennia?

Yes — it's one of our standard game engine options. Just let us know that's what you're using when you sign up and we'll make sure it's configured correctly.

Can I bring my own domain?

Yes. Advanced and Deluxe plans include your own domain name (if available). We can also help with domain transfer and DNS setup.
